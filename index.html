About Us

Our Vision

RIS-GMUN hopes to inspire a generation of young minds to become active global citizens. By providing a platform for students to engage in constructive debates, develop crucial skills, and understand the complexities of international relations, the conference aims to foster a sense of responsibility, empathy, and leadership. RISGMUN strives to ignite a passion for diplomacy and encourage positive contributions towards a better world.

Our Story

Ryan International Ghaziabad Model United Nations conducted at Ryan International School Ghaziabad is an engaging and immersive educational simulation that replicates the workings of the United Nations. RISGMUN is a platform where students can showcase their diplomacy, critical thinking, and public speaking skills while gaining a deeper understanding of global affairs and international relations.

Committees

UNGA

Agenda: Deliberation on the future prospects of space weaponisation with special reference to the intricacies of dual use technologies.

UNSC

Agenda: Discussing the status of the Comprehensive Test Ban Treaty with emphasis on the future of NPT and TPNW in recent geopolitical scenario.

UNHRC

Agenda: The evaluation and prevention of potential human rights violation due to rapid technological development.

WHO

Agenda: Discussing the development of a global pandemic response handbook with international pandemic preparedness.

AIPPM

Agenda: Review of NDA government in past 9 years with special focus on Indian Economy.

G20

Agenda: Deliberation upon the Increasing global warming with special emphasis on the sudden climate change and restoration of the global supply chain.
